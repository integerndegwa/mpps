About Us

Introduction

MPPS pioneered motor vehicle security in Kenya and are still the market leaders in providing motor vehicle related services. Over the years we have partnered with suppliers from different corners of the globe to ensure we deliver an ever expanding and improving range of products to our loyal customers. With the support of our clientele we have now managed to revamp the entire look of our showroom with ample and secure parking, having better machinery, efficient software, happier and more professional staff, and an updated website. We do all these and more in order to give our customers the best experience possible.

Why Choose Us?

UNRIVALLED QUALITY

At MPPS, quality is not just a promise; it's our foundation. We meticulously source products from global leaders, ensuring every item meets the highest international standards. Our commitment to excellence means you receive accessories that are durable, reliable, and perform flawlessly, giving you peace of mind on every journey.

AFTER SALES SUPPORT

Our relationship with you extends far beyond the sale. MPPS prides itself on exceptional after-sales support, offering expert assistance, reliable warranties, and readily available spare parts. Our dedicated team is always on hand to ensure your continued satisfaction and to keep your vehicle secure and well-equipped for years to come.

INDUSTRY LEADER

For decades, MPPS has set the benchmark for motor vehicle security and accessories in Kenya. Our pioneering spirit, continuous innovation, and unwavering dedication to customer satisfaction have solidified our position as the undisputed industry leader. When you choose MPPS, you're choosing experience, expertise, and a legacy of trust.

Our Principles

OUR MISSION

To empower vehicle owners with innovative, high-quality security and accessory solutions, ensuring their safety, comfort, and peace of mind on every journey.

OUR VISION

To be the most trusted and preferred partner for motor vehicle security and accessories in East Africa, continuously expanding our offerings and setting new standards of excellence.

OUR STRATEGY

By fostering strong global partnerships, investing in cutting-edge technology, nurturing a highly professional team, and prioritizing unparalleled customer experience, we aim to maintain our market leadership and drive sustainable growth.
